# 🎨 Knowledge 3D Room - User Guide

## Welcome to Your Immersive Knowledge Experience

The Knowledge Dashboard has been completely transformed into an interactive 3D room where all your knowledge cards are displayed on walls. This guide will help you navigate and explore this immersive experience.

## Getting Started

### First Time Opening
When you open the Knowledge Dashboard, you'll find yourself inside a 3D room with:
- **Front Wall**: Default view with knowledge cards
- **Surrounding Walls**: Additional cards on left, right, and back walls
- **Ceiling & Floor**: Additional visual elements

## Navigation Controls

### Mouse/Trackpad
- **Drag** to rotate the room around
- **Hover cards** to preview them
- **Click cards** to expand and view full content

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- **↑** (Up Arrow) - Tilt room upward (see ceiling)
- **↓** (Down Arrow) - Tilt room downward (see floor)
- **←** (Left Arrow) - Rotate room left
- **→** (Right Arrow) - Rotate room right
- **ESC** - Close expanded card view

### Mobile/Touch
- **Swipe left/right** - Rotate horizontally
- **Swipe up/down** - Rotate vertically
- **Tap card** - Expand to full view
- **Tap close button** - Close expanded view

## UI Elements

### Top-Left Corner 🎨
**Room Information Panel**
- Title: "Immersive Knowledge Experience"
- Subtitle with navigation tips

### Top-Right Corner 📊

- ↑ ↓ ← → buttons for precise room rotation
- Alternative to mouse drag

## Card Features

### Viewing a Card
1. Click any card on the walls
2. Expanded view appears in the center
3. View full card content
4. See bookmark information if applicable

### Bookmark Actions
Cards can have the following actions:

#### Save to Collection
- Click the card to expand
- Automatic bookmark modal appears
- Choose or create a collection
- Set reminder frequency (days)
- Click "Save Bookmark"

#### Mark as Reviewed
- In expanded view, click "Reviewed" button
- Updates your review history
- Resets the reminder timer

#### Remove Bookmark
- In expanded view, click "Remove" button
- Removes from your collections
- Can save again later

### Card Information
Each bookmarked card shows:
- 📁 **Collection** - Which collection it belongs to
- 📅 **Reviewed** - When you last reviewed it
- 🔔 **Reminder** - When you'll be reminded to review
- 🚨 **Due** - If reminder is overdue

## Searching & Filtering

### Search Tips
- Search works across card titles and summaries
- Case-insensitive matching
- Real-time results as you type
- Clear search by X button

### Filter Combinations
- Combine multiple filters
- Filters work across all categories
- Use "Clear filters" button to reset all

### Smart Categories
- **All** shows all cards matching filters
- **Saved** shows only bookmarked cards
- **Due** shows only cards due for review
- Switching category preserves search/filters

## Troubleshooting

### Room Won't Rotate
- Ensure you're clicking and dragging in the center area
- Try keyboard arrows instead
- Check browser console for errors

### Cards Not Showing
- Check if filters are hiding them
- Try clearing all filters
- Ensure search is empty

### Expanded Card Won't Close
- Press ESC key
- Click the X button in top-right of expanded card
- Click outside the card modal

### Remind Me Later Button Not Working
- Ensure you have permissions to create bookmarks
- Check that a collection name or days are specified
- Try refreshing the page
